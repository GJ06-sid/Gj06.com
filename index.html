— the people behind the chai —

OUR STORY

panel 01
👤

It started with a cup.

GJ 06 was born from a single obsession — the perfect cup of chai. Not the rushed, bag-dipped kind. The real kind. The slow kind. The kind that makes you sit down and breathe.

panel 02
🍞

Then came the bread.

The chai needed a companion. So we baked. And baked. And kept baking until every loaf felt like a hug. Our bakehouse runs 5am every morning, before the city wakes up.

panel 03
✦

The 2D world appeared.

Why 2D? Because life is complicated enough. In our cafe, everything is flat, clear, and honest. What you see is what you get. No depth required.
